The Transformer System

Verb Tenses

Keep -el ending.

  • Past: -if
  • Future: -ma
Noun Conversion

Change -el to -enta.

Example: vadilitel (do) → vadilitenta (job)

Adjective Conversion

Drop the final -l.

Example: daystel (think) → daysto (smart)

Inversions & Doers

Add -poe (not) or -nica (person).

Example: passelnica (teacher)
